---
title: "Step-by-Step"
---

Here are the simple steps to convert your Figma design into a working App.

1. Login to Symplr Studio
2. Select Import from Figma
3. Login to Figma and select your design
4. Wait for the Import to complete
5. Explore the App Blueprint
6. Build & Run the App
7. Download the Code
8. Build & Run Automated Tests
