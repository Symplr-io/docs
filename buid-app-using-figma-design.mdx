### **Convert Your Figma Design into a Working App (UI)**

The Symplr Figma Agent turns your designs into a real, working app.

Instead of spending time rebuilding what you’ve already designed, you can instantly bring your Figma screens to life as a functional interface—ready to use, refine, and evolve.

From a static design to a living product, in minutes.

**What you get:**

- A working app based on your Figma designs
- Interfaces that match your layouts and visual style
- A foundation you can immediately build on and improve

**Why it matters:**

- Go from idea to working product faster
- Eliminate the need to recreate designs manually
- Keep your vision intact from design to final experience

Think of the Symplr Figma Agent as the fastest way to move from design to reality—where what you create in Figma becomes something you can actually use.
