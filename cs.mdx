Need help with Symplr? We’re here to support you—every step of the way.

Whether you’re troubleshooting an issue, exploring features, or looking for guidance, our support team is ready to help you move forward quickly and confidently.

## 🧭 When to Contact Support

Reach out to us anytime you need assistance with:

- Errors or unexpected behavior in your application
- Features not working as expected
- Issues with Figma import or Blueprint View
- Configuration, workflows, or integration challenges
- Account or access-related concerns

## ⚡ Report Issues Instantly—Right from the Application

Symplr makes it easy to report issues the moment they occur—without interrupting your workflow.

- When an error appears, a **“Contact Support”** button is displayed directly within the message
- Click **“Contact Support”** to instantly report the issue

Once submitted, Symplr automatically captures and sends key technical details to our support team, including:

- **Application ID**
- **Error code**
- **Error information**
- **Customer details**

These details are automatically emailed to our support team, enabling faster diagnosis and resolution—without requiring manual input from you.

## 🔍 Resolve Issues Faster

Before reaching out, you may find a quick solution by exploring:

- Product documentation and guides
- The FAQ section
- Blogs and product updates for tips, best practices, and known issues

💡 Many common issues can be resolved instantly using these resources.

## 📩 How to Contact Support

### 💬 In-App Support

Get real-time assistance directly within Symplr:

1. Click the **Help** button
2. Start a chat with our support team
3. Share your issue along with relevant details (e.g., screenshots, steps, or error messages)

⚡ Ideal for quick questions and general guidance.

### 📧 Email Support

For more detailed queries or when attachments are required, contact us via email:

[**support@101digital.io**](mailto:support@101digital.io)

## 📋 Additional Details (if needed)

When contacting support via chat or email, providing additional context can help us resolve your issue faster:

- Project / App Name
- Description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce
- Screenshots or recordings (if applicable)

💡 When using the **Contact Support** button, most technical details are captured automatically.

## ⏱️ Response Time

Our team is committed to providing timely support:

- **24–48 hours** for general queries
- **Priority response** for critical or blocking issues

<Note>
  Critical issues include application crashes, build failures, or blockers that prevent you from continuing your work.
</Note>
