title: "Contact Support"
---

Need help with Symplr? Our support team is available to assist you with any issues, questions, or feedback.

Whether you are experiencing a technical problem or need guidance, you can contact support to get assistance quickly.

---

## 🧭 When to Contact Support

Contact support if you experience any of the following:

- Errors or unexpected behavior in the application
- Features not working as expected
- Issues with app configuration, workflows, or integrations
- Problems with Figma import or **Storyboard View**
- Account or access-related issues

---

## 🔍 Before You Contact Support

Before reaching out, try the following:

- Review the relevant documentation and guides
- Check the FAQ section
- Explore Blogs and Updates for tips, best practices, and known issues

> 💡 Checking these resources may help you resolve common issues faster.

---

## 📩 How to Contact Support

### 💬 In-App Support (Recommended)

Use the **Help** button within the application for quick assistance:

1. Click the **Help** button
2. Start a chat with the support team
3. Describe your issue and provide relevant details

> ⚡ This is the fastest way to get help for general questions or quick guidance.

---

### 📧 Email Support

For detailed issues or when attachments are required, contact support via email:

[**support@symplr.io**](mailto:support@symplr.io)

---

## 📋 Information to Include

To help resolve your issue quickly, include the following:

- **Project / App Name**
- **Description of the issue**
- **Steps to reproduce the problem**
- **Expected vs actual behavior**
- **Screenshots or screen recordings** (if applicable)

> ⚠️ Providing complete information helps us investigate and respond more efficiently.

---

## ⏱️ Response Time

Our support team typically responds within:

- **24–48 hours** for general queries
- **Faster for critical issues**

---

## 🐞 Reporting Bugs

When reporting a bug, include:

- A clear description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Any error messages (if available)
- Screenshots or recordings

> 💡 Detailed reports help us resolve issues faster.

---

## 🙌 We’re Here to Help

Your feedback is valuable to us and helps improve Symplr. If you need assistance, don’t hesitate to reach out — we’re here to support you.
