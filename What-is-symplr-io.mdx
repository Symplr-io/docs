---
title: "What is Symplr.io"
---

[Symplr.io](https://symplr.io) is an AI-powered app builder that enables a new way of bring ideas to life—where intelligent AI capabilities help shape your vision into a working web or mobile application.

Instead of navigating fragmented tools and slow processes, you work in a unified environment where your product takes shape in real time. You define what to create, and Symplr translates that intent into a functional application.

With Symplr, you can:

- Import Figma designs and convert them into applications  
- Generate applications using AI prompts  
- Start with pre-built templates  
- Build, test, and run applications instantly  
- Download and extend your code  

At the center of this experience is the **Blueprint**—a visual representation of your application. It captures your screens, navigation, and logic, acting as a single source of truth from which your application is built. As your Blueprint evolves, your application evolves with it.

**Simple to start. Transparent to understand. Powerful to evolve.**

With [Symplr.io](https://symplr.io) you’re not just building—you’re guiding your product as it comes to life.

**Explore the documentation to learn how to turn your ideas or designs into a fully functional application.**
