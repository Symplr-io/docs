Need assistance with Symplr? Our support team is available to help you resolve issues, answer questions, and ensure a smooth experience with the platform.

Whether you are facing a technical issue or require guidance, you can contact support for timely and reliable assistance.

## 🧭 When to Contact Support

Please contact support if you encounter any of the following:

- Errors or unexpected behavior within the application
- Features not functioning as expected
- Issues related to Figma import or Blueprint View
- Challenges with configuration, workflows, or integrations
- Account or access-related concerns

## ⚡ Report Issues Directly from the Application

Symplr provides a built-in mechanism to report issues efficiently at the point of occurrence.

- When an error message appears, a **“Contact Support”** button is displayed within the interface
- Click the **“Contact Support”** button to submit the issue

Upon submission, Symplr automatically captures and sends relevant technical details to the support team, including:

- **Application ID**
- **Error code**
- **Error information**
- **Customer details**

These details are automatically forwarded to the support team, enabling faster analysis and resolution without requiring additional manual input.

## 🔍 Before You Contact Support

Before reaching out, we recommend reviewing the following resources:

- Product documentation and user guides
- Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
- Blogs and product updates for known issues and best practices

💡 These resources may help resolve common issues without the need for further assistance.

## 📩 How to Contact Support

### 💬 In-App Support

Access support directly within the application:

1. Click the **Help** button
2. Initiate a chat with the support team
3. Provide details of your issue, including screenshots, steps, or error messages

This option is suitable for general queries and non-critical issues.

### 📧 Email Support

For more detailed queries or when attachments are required, contact support via email:

[**support@101digital.io**](mailto:support@101digital.io)

## 📋 Additional Details (if required)

When contacting support via chat or email, providing the following information will assist in faster resolution:

- Project / App Name
- Description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Screenshots or recordings (if applicable)

💡 When using the **Contact Support** button, most technical details are captured automatically.

## ⏱️ Response Time

Our support team aims to respond within:

- **24–48 hours** for general queries
- **Priority response times** for critical or blocking issues

<Note>
  Critical issues include application crashes, build failures, or blockers that prevent continued work.
</Note>

## 🙌 Support Commitment

We are committed to providing reliable and efficient support to ensure your success with Symplr.

If you require assistance, please reach out to our support team—we are here to help.
