Need help with Symplr? Our support team is available to assist you with any issues, questions, or feedback.

Whether you are experiencing a technical problem or need guidance, you can contact support to get assistance quickly and efficiently.

## 🧭 When to Contact Support

Please contact support if you experience any of the following:

- Errors or unexpected behavior in the application
- Features not working as expected
- Issues with Figma import or Blueprint View
- Problems with configuration, workflows, or integrations
- Account or access-related issues

## ⚡ Report Issues Directly from the Application

Symplr provides a built-in way to report issues instantly when they occur.

- When an error message appears, a **“Report Issue”** button is displayed
- Click the **“Report Issue”** button to submit the issue directly
- Provide any additional details if prompted

This allows you to report issues immediately without leaving your current workflow, helping our team investigate and resolve problems faster.

## 📋 Information to Include

To help resolve your issue quickly, include the following:

- **Project / App Name**
- **Project / App Link**
- **Description of the issue**
- **Steps to reproduce the problem**
- **Expected vs actual behavior**
- **Screenshots or screen recordings** (if applicable)

⚠️ Providing complete information helps us investigate and respond more efficiently.

## 🔍 Before You Contact Support

Before contacting support, try the following:

- Review the relevant documentation and guides
- Check the FAQ section
- Explore blogs and product updates for tips, best practices, and known issues

💡 Checking these resources may help you resolve common issues faster.

## 📩 How to Contact Support

### 💬 In-App Support

Use the **Help** button within the application for quick assistance:

1. Click the **Help** button
2. Start a chat with the support team
3. Describe your issue and include relevant details (e.g., screenshots, steps, or error messages)

⚡ This option is best for general questions or non-critical issues.

### 📧 Email Support

For detailed issues or when attachments are required, contact support via email:

[**support@101digital.io**](mailto:support@101digital.io)

## ⏱️ Response Time

Our support team typically responds within:

- **24–48 hours** for general queries
- **Faster response times for critical or blocking issues**

<Note>
  Critical issues include application crashes, build failures, or blockers that prevent you from continuing your work.
</Note>

## 🙌 We’re Here to Help

Your feedback is valuable to us and helps improve Symplr. If you need assistance, feel free to reach out — we’re here to help you succeed with Symplr.
